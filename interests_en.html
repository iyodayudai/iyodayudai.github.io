The Wonders of Metabolism

Design principles of how organisms build body from food

Overview Details

Living organisms—from humans to dogs, insects, and plants—grow by taking in food. Inside their bodies, food is broken down and used to build the materials of the body such as muscle, bone, and skin. This system is called metabolism, which functions like a factory inside living organisms.

My research aims to explore the mysteries surrounding metabolism.
